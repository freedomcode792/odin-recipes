Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a mexican avocado salad. It is a popular in the North America as a dip eaten with mexican tortilla chips.

This is a wonderful dish for lazy cooks (such as myself), due to ease of cooking and great taste!

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.

  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
